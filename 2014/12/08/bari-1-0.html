bari 1.0 released

08 Dec 2014

I already wrote about bari in May.

As a reminder, bari is a build management system primarily for .NET, trying to fix Visual Studio's bad parts while keeping the good ones.

After more than two years of development, and being in production at KOTEM for almost half year, bari reached a state when it can be considered as a stable and usable first version.

To indicate this today I released bari 1.0.

Try it out and feel free to give any kind of feedback or ask any question!
